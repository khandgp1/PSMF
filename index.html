03 — Training Adjustments

Same intensity. Less volume.

At 2x/week, the goal during a PSMF isn't progress — it's signal preservation. Heavy, brief, and done.

Keep

  • Heavy compound lifts
  • Working intensity / load
  • Session frequency (2x/week)

Reduce

  • Total volume by 30–50%
  • Accessory / junk sets
  • Training to failure
4–5 working sets → 2–3 hard working sets — maintain strength, don't chase growth.

06 — What To Expect

14-day outlook

Week 1
  • Scale down 3–6 lb
  • Mostly glycogen and water
  • Some genuine fat loss begins
Week 2
  • Fat loss becomes the main driver
  • Strength may dip slightly
After Refeed
  • Weight +2–5 lb (glycogen/water)
  • Fuller muscles, better pumps
  • Improved strength and energy
Outcome (10–14 days)
  • 2–4 lb actual fat lost
  • Noticeably leaner appearance
  • ~9–10% body fat, depending on water
Reassess

Repeat the 6–7 day PSMF / 1 refeed cycle for 2–4 weeks total, then reassess body composition, training performance, and whether to continue, hold at maintenance, or transition to a slower deficit.
